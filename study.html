HORIZON - Travel App

Discovering local activities and planning road trips just got easier! Our platform helps travelers find and book tickets quickly, while seamlessly planning exciting activities along the way. Whether you're exploring new destinations or enjoying a spontaneous adventure, we've got you covered.

Problem Statement

Current travel apps often fail to account for the fact that users typically do not invest much time in researching or planning their trips until after they have already arrived at their destination.

Wireframe & Sketches

Upon finalizing the User Flow, I began developing the wireframes for the mobile application. My main objective was to understand user interactions from the main screen and determine the most relevant information users would seek based on their current location. This aimed to create an intuitive and efficient user experience.

Low-Fidelity Prototype
